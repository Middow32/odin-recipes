World's Best Lasagna Recipe


Description

This is the best lasagna that you will ever make. No other lasagna could possibly compare to this recipe.
The recipe has been passed down in the family through several generations.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion/Garlic
  • Tomate products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving
