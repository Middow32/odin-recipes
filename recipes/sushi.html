Smoked Salmon Sushi Recipe


Description

This is a very basic and simple to make Smoked Salmon Sushi recipe.
It may take some practice to make initially.
However, with time, you'll become a smoked sushi making mastermind!

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice
  • Rice wine vinegar
  • Seaweed
  • Wasabi paste
  • Smoked salmon
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Soak rice
  2. Cook rice
  3. Mix vinegar and cool in an even layer on a plate
  4. Assemble the sushi rolls
  5. Roll the sushi tightly and cut into eight pieces
